### Material

> **Watering hole** is a [computer attack](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Attack_\(computing\) "Attack (computing)") strategy in which an attacker guesses or observes which websites an organization often uses and infects one or more of them with [malware](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malware "Malware"). Eventually, some member of the targeted group will become infected. Additionally, websites can enhance protection by disabling or removing vulnerable software, such as Flash and Adobe Reader, which are commonly targeted in cyber attacks.
