### Quotes

> [**Guide: Scenes**](https://clevergirlhelps.tumblr.com/post/98233954157/guide-scenes) A helpful guide with suggestions on how to outline what happens in the scenes and chapters of a story.
> 
> [**Tips on Keeping Your Scenes Engaging**](https://www.tumblr.com/heywriters/728043775721652224/tips-on-keeping-your-scenes-engaging?source=share) A detailed post with helpful suggestions for writing engaging scenes. Includes a short list of resources for further help with writing scenes.
> 
> **[How to Make the Most of Your Scenes](https://www.writerswrite.co.za/how-to-make-the-most-of-your-scenes/)** An article that offers advice for how to write scenes by using a method called “the cell phone reaction.”
> 
> [**How to Write a Scene in 11 Steps**](https://thewritingrealm.tumblr.com/post/89332002980) An infographic with a list of things to consider (or do) when writing a scene.
> 
> [**If You’re Stuck on a Chapter**](https://www.tumblr.com/fixyourwritinghabits/762169261394362368?source=share) A post with helpful suggestions for what to do when you’re stuck on a chapter or a scene.
> 
> [**No Such Thing as Filler**](https://www.tumblr.com/fixyourwritinghabits/748318008921948160/no-such-thing-as-filler?source=share) A post that explains what filler episodes are, and how filler scenes in writing can help move the story forward.
> 
> **[How to Make a Scene More Heartfelt](https://www.tumblr.com/heywriters/721962446300086272/how-to-make-a-scene-more-heartfelt?source=share)** A post with suggestions on how to write a more heartfelt scene. Written specifically with romantic scenes in mind, but provides generally good suggestions for writing any kind of tender or emotional scene.
> 
> **[Tips for Writing Interrupted Scenes](https://www.tumblr.com/elumish/722510694583549952?source=share)** Provides suggestions for how to write scenes that get interrupted, and how to avoid plot holes when writing interrupted scenes.
> 
> [**Subtle Scene Transitions**](https://writingquestionsanswered.tumblr.com/post/181986266751/i-really-need-to-work-on-my-transitions-i-have) A helpful post that offers advice on how to write the transitions between scenes or chapters.
