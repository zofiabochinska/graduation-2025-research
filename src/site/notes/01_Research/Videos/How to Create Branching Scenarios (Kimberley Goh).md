#### Tip 1: Don't Use the Time Cave Branching

![](https://i.imgur.com/jBKg7sj.png)

Uncontrolled branching which grows exponentially and can quickly get out of control. Most people are impatient and won't play 27 times just to experience all 27 endings. So, you could be wasting a lot of time and resources on a little reward.
#### Tip 2: Use Bottlenecks

![](https://i.imgur.com/0WqYaT5.png)

A bottleneck is an event that happens no matter what choice the player has made before. It limits the amount of endings and choices you have to make.
