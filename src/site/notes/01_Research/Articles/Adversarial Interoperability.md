# Notes
---

> For a really competitive, innovative, dynamic marketplace, you need _adversarial_ interoperability: that’s when you create a new product or service that plugs into the existing ones _without the permission_ of the companies that make them. Think of third-party printer ink, alternative app stores, or independent repair shops that use compatible parts from rival manufacturers to fix your car or your phone or your tractor.

> Big Tech climbed the adversarial ladder and then pulled it up behind them.
