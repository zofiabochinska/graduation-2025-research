### Thoughts
 
 It's made in Ren'Py and in the credits section (Break room) it mentions the assets and SFX and music they used:

[Sound Effects](https://www.zapsplat.com/sound-effect-categories/)
[Music](http://www.hmix.net/music_gallery/music_top.htm)
[Horror Music](http://www.presence-of-music.com/horror_suspence.html)

- The title card is too busy for me, same with the settings.

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/OVJJjPk.png)
![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/QK8Jibr.jpg)

- When the script switches from dialogue to narration, the background gets blurry.

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/eOYlhKY.png)

-  Sometimes to show emotions, the scene shakes.
- There is a typewriter sound when the characters speak. 
- Names of different characters have different colours to differentiate them.

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/3rqRBNr.jpg)

- The background picture is still and sporadically the camera zooms in to make the scene more dynamic or intimate, or it pans to the left slowly showing something further. 

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/vkkeUss.jpg)

- When there is another place we are going to, there is a chapter card that says "Diner 7:00 AM" etc. to let the player know where they are right now. 

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/cmsEX6h.png)

- The camera pans over to show the place while you can read the narration. 
- There is a light gradient from the bottom so that the text is more visible.

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/L7gEaNS.jpg)

- When the character is thinking it's written as italics. When they are speaking it's in quotation marks. 

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/EFWK30p.jpg)

- Objects are shown as a card in the middle of the screen with the background blurry. It makes sense because you don't have to draw the characters holding the object each time. 

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/ECc7nGc.jpg)
![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/92Y4qFz.png)

- Only the face of the character changes to indicate emotion change and add dynamics.

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/JA6hZ5Y.jpg)

- The same character art is shown in another place, just the background changes.

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/jQNnObo.jpg)

- The characters look at us even though we are not a separate character in the game. The dynamic between them are also shown based on where they are positioned on the screen.

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/lZZKtL8.jpg)

- The action is shown through the sound instead of animation. Like a rustling of paper when a letter is being opened. 

- The choice is very simple. When I chose "Reject the case." it made me go to the bad ending. I wish there was a save made automatically or a button that would let me go back in time and make another choice then instead of having to start over. 

![Imgur](https://i.imgur.com/MsoTAE6.png)
