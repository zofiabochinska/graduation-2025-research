### Thoughts
 
 This game was made in Ren'py which is great news for me. It uses a lot of music and sound effects, which sets the scene very well. I really like the style, because it's quite grungy and combines illustration with pictures (it seems like). The walking system with buttons is a bit confusing to me and could be improved but it's a solid base and it works. I like that there is a tutorial that explains how everything works to the player. The menu[^3] is also very simple which is nice. I like the inventory system[^1] and the point and click mechanic that let's you interact with the game - including the interactions you can choose with the characters[^2].
