### Thoughts
 
Really nice menu[^1] - this VN was a collaboration between 5 people - one lead (who also did programming and writing), one editor and writer, one artist, one music producer and one voice actor. I think I would be open to collaborating with another artist for the art, maybe write songs with my boyfriend or ask him to do it and could reach out to one of my friends for the voice acting. 
 
I really liked the chat functionality[^2] actually. Maybe I should reach out to ask about the code for it or how it was written in Ren'Py[^3]? I really liked it and it sounded very realistic and each of the characters had a distinct style of typing. I also loved the mini games at the sides, even though the learning curve was a bit steep at first. 

I love the idea of a voice call[^4] and that this is when more of the interaction happens and you can deepen your relationship with someone this way and exit the chat. It breaks apart the game in a good way. 

I do not think I would like to incorporate a love interest in my game, as I do not want it to be the main part of the game (and I feel like it could become that then). However, I could add close friends, family members, coworkers etc.
